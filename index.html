The Mayan communities of the Chuj ethnicity settled around the lake offer several activities that allow visitors to enjoy Laugna Brava's natural beauty.

The Lake Challenge

Put your strengths to work by crossing the four kilometers in a kayak guided by a local guide. This adventure is physically demanding and reccommended for only those in excellent physical condition.

  • Duration: 5 hours approx.
  • Price: $50 USD.
  • Minimum: 2 pax.
